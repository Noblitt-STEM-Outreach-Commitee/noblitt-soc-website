About Our Committee

Who We Are

Part of the prestigious Noblitt Scholars Program at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, our Social Outreach Committee consists of highly motivated students dedicated to fostering STEM education in our community.

Our Mission

We expand K-12 STEM curiosity and confidence through engaging, hands-on activities that make science, technology, engineering, and mathematics accessible and exciting for young learners.
